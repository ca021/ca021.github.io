Study Abroad 2020

Edinburgh, Scotland

Personal Goals

Through my study abroad opportunity, I hoped to learn about a new culture and experience life in their world. I wanted to see what they face daily, their successes and problems, their foods, music and family life. I was excited to see how my major, Computer Science and Engineering, is used in another country. I wanted to observe and learn about their technology and benefit from what and how they teach. This was another way for me to learn more about and appreciate diversity. I heard different languages and met other students from around the world, comparing our similarities and differences, learning what is important to them.

Why Edinburgh?

I will admit, when my mom suggested a school in Scotland, I had no background knowledge on the country. When I thought of Scotland I thought of kilts and constant rain. I did not want to spend my semester in a gloomy, rain city overseas. Thankfully, my mom and advisor at Bucknell really pushed for a school in Scotland. Scotland universities have been recognized for their teaching excellence. Specifically, the University of Edinburgh is one of the best schools for Informatics in the United Kingdom. They offered a wide variety of courses that fit my requirements to stay on track at Bucknell University. Edinburgh turned out to be the perfect fit for me!

Courtney Andree, Computer Science & Engineering
